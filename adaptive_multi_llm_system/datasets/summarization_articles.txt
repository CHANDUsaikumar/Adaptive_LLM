The quick brown fox is a well-known pangram in English: a short sentence that contains every letter of the alphabet at least once. Pangrams are often used to display fonts, test typewriters and keyboards, and in teaching handwriting. They serve designers and typographers by providing concise examples of diverse letter combinations.
